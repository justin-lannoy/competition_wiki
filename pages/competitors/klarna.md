# Klarna Group

## Overview

Klarna (KLAR) is a global BNPL platform that IPO'd in late 2025. Limited current direct overlap with Snap's verticals (Klarna's US business skews softer goods, fashion, and travel), but a relevant signal for POS-financing investor sentiment, regulatory posture, and underwriting trends across the broader category.
