# Koalafi

## Overview

Koalafi is a private POS-financing platform offering both a prime credit waterfall (via bank partners) and a lease-to-own backstop, packaged as a single merchant-facing application. Strong concentration in furniture, mattresses, and home goods. The Synchrony Business Center bundle (live since January 2026) is the most strategically significant 2026 development in the LTO/POS space — it pairs Synchrony's prime card with Koalafi's non-prime waterfall in a single onboarding flow.

## Recent moves

- [[2026-05-20-koalafi-michigan-ag-settlement]] — Michigan Attorney General Dana Nessel secured an Assurance of Voluntary Compliance and Discontinuance with West Creek Financial d/b/a Koalafi, resolving allegations that the company violated the Michigan Consumer Protection Act and Rental-Purchase Agreement Act (high · regulatory)

## Read-through themes

- **Bundled prime + non-prime stack** — The most direct strategic threat in the 2026 dataset: a single-application offer that delivers both prime and non-prime decisioning. This pressures single-product fintechs (whether prime-only or non-prime-only) most acutely in furniture/mattress where Synchrony has long-standing merchant relationships.
- **Speed-to-market** — Koalafi went from an LTO-only player to a bundled offer in roughly 12 months. Expect the bundle to expand to more verticals through 2026.

## Notes

- Private; no IR; primary signals come from merchant-association coverage (HomeWorld Business, Furniture Today) and partner press releases (Synchrony, KeyBank as a backer).
- Snap's defensive posture: emphasize speed-to-decision and merchant-economics where Snap can demonstrate higher attach on declines than the Koalafi bundle delivers.
