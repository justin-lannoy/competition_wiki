# Progressive Leasing

## Overview

Progressive Leasing is the lease-to-own arm of PROG Holdings (NASDAQ: PRG), Snap Finance's most direct enterprise-tier LTO competitor. Strong concentration in furniture, mattresses, and tire/auto verticals; aggressive on enterprise wins. The recent acquisition of Purchasing Power expands PROG into the payroll-deduct/employer benefits channel — a new attack surface for non-prime credit at the workplace.


## Read-through themes

- **Recovery is real** — Q1 2026 GMV inflecting positive is the strongest signal in 2 years that PROG's demand environment has stabilized. Expect the sales team to be more aggressive in enterprise prospecting and retention through 2026 H2.
- **Employer channel expansion** — Purchasing Power gives Progressive a workplace-benefits foothold Snap doesn't currently address. Watch for PROG cross-selling traditional LTO into Purchasing Power's employer base.
- **Direct enterprise overlap** — Progressive remains the most likely competitor in any large enterprise RFP Snap pursues, especially in furniture/mattress and tires/auto.
