# American First Finance (AFF)

## Overview

AFF is the financing segment of FirstCash Holdings (NASDAQ: FCFS), competing with Snap Finance in lease-to-own and second-look credit across furniture, tires, auto repair, and home goods. AFF reports as a segment within FCFS, so the consolidated print includes pawn-store earnings; the segment-level disclosures are where the LTO-relevant signal sits.

## Read-through themes

- **Loss curve bending the wrong way at the margin** — A single-quarter +$7.6M provision charge isn't decisive on its own, but it's the largest segment-level deterioration AFF has flagged in recent prints. Possible UW or pricing actions ahead, which historically translate to a tighter approval rate and decline-back volume that Snap can pursue.
- **Bank-partner OBS structure under pressure** — The provision sits inside the bank-partner channel, not direct AFF originations. If sustained, it could prompt structural changes to the OBS partnership terms — a slow-motion vulnerability for AFF's bank-partner pipeline.
