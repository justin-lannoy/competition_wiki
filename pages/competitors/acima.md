# Acima

## Overview

Acima is a lease-to-own platform owned by Upbound Group (NASDAQ: UPBD), the same parent that operates Rent-A-Center and Brigit. Acima competes directly with Snap Finance for non-prime POS originations across furniture, mattresses, appliances, tires, and auto repair.


## Read-through themes

- **Quality over volume** — Acima is choosing portfolio quality over volume for the second consecutive quarter. That widens share-of-merchant opportunity for Snap on the originations Acima is now declining.
- **Bob's Discount Furniture exclusive (Dec 2025)** — Acima retains a major furniture-vertical anchor; Snap should monitor cross-sell behavior between the Bob's Discount Furniture relationship and any Snap-served Bob's-adjacent merchants.

## Notes

- Public via parent UPBD; Q1 prints typically late April / early May.
- Distinct competitor surface from sibling Rent-A-Center (which competes more in traditional retail) — Acima is the POS/embedded play.
