# Acima

## Overview

Acima is a lease-to-own platform owned by Upbound Group (NASDAQ: UPBD), the same parent that operates Rent-A-Center and Brigit. Acima competes directly with Snap Finance for non-prime POS originations across furniture, mattresses, appliances, tires, and auto repair.
