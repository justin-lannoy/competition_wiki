# Affirm Holdings

## Overview

Affirm (NASDAQ: AFRM) is a leading buy-now-pay-later platform. Less direct head-to-head with Snap on lease-to-own, but increasingly relevant on POS credit decisioning and merchant-side share-of-checkout dynamics. The relevant Snap-side risk is Affirm-as-prime-waterfall pushing Snap further down the merchant's stack and crowding out non-prime decline-back-to-Snap volume.
