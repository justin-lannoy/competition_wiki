# Affirm Holdings

## Overview

Affirm (NASDAQ: AFRM) is a leading buy-now-pay-later platform. Less direct head-to-head with Snap on lease-to-own, but increasingly relevant on POS credit decisioning and merchant-side share-of-checkout dynamics. The relevant Snap-side risk is Affirm-as-prime-waterfall pushing Snap further down the merchant's stack and crowding out non-prime decline-back-to-Snap volume.


## Read-through themes

- **Beat would legitimize BNPL adjacency** — A clean print plus raised guide tends to crowd in more merchant-acquisition spend across BNPL peers (Klarna, PayPal Pay-in-4), tightening checkout share for second-look products like Snap. Expect more aggressive auto/home merchant pursuit.
- **Decisioning model is the choke point** — Affirm's prime waterfall increasingly competes for Snap's merchant slot before the customer ever reaches non-prime. Watch underwriting commentary in earnings for any tightening that would re-open mid-tier decline-back to Snap.

## Notes

- Public; fiscal year ends June, so May earnings = fiscal Q3.
- Limited direct vertical overlap in Snap's enterprise book today, but every Affirm enterprise win at a Snap merchant is a pre-Snap waterfall that thins decline volume.
