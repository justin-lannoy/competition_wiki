### 2026-05-13 — Yahoo Finance Singapore

**Scratch Financial, Inc. announces purchase facility with Victory Park Capital to expand technology platform that offers financing options for veterinary care**

Scratchpay secured a new purchase facility (debt financing) from Victory Park Capital to scale its veterinary-focused BNPL/financing platform, signaling continued investor appetite for vertical-specific point-of-sale lending. For Snap Finance, this reinforces that the pet/vet care vertical is attracting dedicated capital and competition, though Scratchpay's narrow veterinary focus limits direct overlap with Snap's broader retail lease-to-own model.
