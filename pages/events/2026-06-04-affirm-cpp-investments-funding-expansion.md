# Affirm Renews and Expands CPP Investments Forward-Flow Agreement to $2.2B, Targeting $8B in Loan Volume

## What happened

Affirm and Canada Pension Plan Investment Board announced a renewed and expanded 24-month forward-flow agreement on June 4, 2026. CPP Investments committed $1.7B to purchasing Affirm installment loans with the ability to increase to $2.2B, expected to support approximately $8B in consumer loan volume over two years. Since 2019, CPP Investments has purchased nearly $14B in Affirm assets, and Affirm's total funding capacity now stands at $28.2B.

## Read-through for Snap

Affirm's deepening institutional capital base at scale ($28B capacity) widens its cost-of-funds advantage over Snap Finance, enabling more aggressive zero-APR merchant subsidies and underwriting expansion.
