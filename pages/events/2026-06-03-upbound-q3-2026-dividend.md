# Upbound Group Declares $0.39 Q3 2026 Quarterly Dividend, Signals Financial Stability

## What happened

Upbound Group (NASDAQ: UPBD), parent of Acima, declared a $0.39-per-share quarterly cash dividend for Q3 2026, payable July 7, 2026 to stockholders of record as of June 16, 2026. The dividend yield stands at approximately 8.38% and represents the fourth consecutive year of dividend increases. This follows Q1 2026 results where Acima revenue rose ~1.8% YoY to $649M amid deliberate underwriting tightening.

## Read-through for Snap

Upbound's continued dividend growth signals balance-sheet confidence despite Acima GMV headwinds from tighter underwriting, suggesting the competitor remains well-capitalized to invest in merchant acquisition and platform development.
