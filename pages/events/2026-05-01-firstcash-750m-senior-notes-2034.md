# FirstCash Completes Upsized $750M Senior Notes Offering at 6.125% Due 2034

## What happened

FirstCash Holdings closed a privately placed $750M offering of 6.125% senior unsecured notes due May 1, 2034, upsized from an initial $600M. Proceeds are intended to repay existing indebtedness and provide liquidity for future growth and general corporate purposes. The offering was sold to qualified institutional buyers under Rule 144A and Reg S.

## Read-through for Snap

Refinancing and liquidity buildup at FirstCash (parent of American First Finance) strengthens AFF's ability to invest in merchant growth, technology, and competitive pricing, raising the stakes for Snap in the LTO/retail-finance space.
