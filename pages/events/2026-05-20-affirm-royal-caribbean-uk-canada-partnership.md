# Affirm Partners with Royal Caribbean for Flexible Installment Payments in UK and Canada

## What happened

On May 20, 2026, Affirm announced a new partnership with Royal Caribbean allowing UK and Canadian holidaymakers to spread cruise booking costs over time with no compounding interest or late fees. The deal expands Affirm's geographic footprint beyond North America into the travel and hospitality vertical, a high-ticket purchase category.

## Read-through for Snap

Affirm's continued international and vertical expansion into travel reinforces its merchant network breadth and brand reach, though its prime-leaning customer base remains largely distinct from Snap Finance's non-prime LTO segment.
