# Klarna Launches 'Klarna Inbox' In-App Security Feature to Combat Impersonation Scams Globally

## What happened

On June 4, 2026, Klarna launched a new in-app security feature called Klarna Inbox, which mirrors all official communications from the company to customers—including emails, SMS, push notifications, and physical letters—enabling consumers to verify any message claiming to be from Klarna. The feature is now live across all markets globally. The launch comes as Klarna reported Q1 2026 revenue of $1.0B (+44% YoY) and 119 million active consumers.

## Read-through for Snap

By deepening consumer trust and app stickiness across 119M+ active users globally, Klarna reinforces its platform lock-in and brand authority in digital payments, making it harder for alternative financing providers like Snap to capture share among digitally-savvy consumers.
