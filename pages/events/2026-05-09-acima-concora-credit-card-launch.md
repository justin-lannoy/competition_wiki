# Acima Launches Co-Branded Mastercard and Private Label Credit Card via Concora Partnership

## What happened

Upbound Group introduced the Acima Classic Credit General-Purpose Mastercard and the Acima Private Label Credit Card through a partnership with Concora, extending Acima's product lineup beyond lease-to-own into revolving credit. The move is intended to expand financial access for Acima's customer base and drive incremental revenue and customer base growth.

## Read-through for Snap

Acima's entry into co-branded credit products creates a more complete financing waterfall that directly competes with Snap Finance's non-prime and near-prime customer acquisition funnel.
