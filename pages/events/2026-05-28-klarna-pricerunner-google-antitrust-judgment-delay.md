# Swedish Court Delays PriceRunner v. Google Antitrust Judgment to June 10, 2026

## What happened

Klarna updated investors that the Patent and Market Court in Stockholm rescheduled delivery of its judgment in the PriceRunner v. Google antitrust damages case from April 15 to June 10, 2026, citing the need for additional time to finalize the judgment. Klarna cautioned that outcomes are uncertain, any award could be appealed, and the result is subject to tax and sharing arrangements.

## Read-through for Snap

A favorable ruling could provide Klarna an unexpected capital injection, potentially accelerating its U.S. expansion and intensifying competition with Snap Finance.
