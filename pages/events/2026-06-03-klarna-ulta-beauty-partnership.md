# Klarna Partners with Ulta Beauty to Bring Flexible Payments to U.S. Beauty Shoppers

## What happened

Klarna announced a partnership with Ulta Beauty on June 3, 2026, bringing its flexible payment options to U.S. beauty shoppers. The deal continues Klarna's aggressive U.S. merchant acquisition push, which has grown its merchant base to over one million globally, up 49% year-over-year. Klarna's U.S. GMV grew 39% YoY in Q1 2026.

## Read-through for Snap

Klarna's rapidly expanding U.S. merchant roster in specialty retail increases competitive pressure on Snap Finance's mid-market merchant base, particularly among overlapping consumer demographics.
