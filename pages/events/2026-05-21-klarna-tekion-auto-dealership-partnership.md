# Klarna Partners With Tekion to Bring Flexible Payments to Auto Dealerships

## What happened

Klarna announced a partnership with Tekion, a cloud-based automotive retail platform, to bring Klarna's flexible payment options to auto dealerships in the U.S. The integration expands Klarna's merchant network into the automotive services vertical, adding another point-of-sale financing channel beyond traditional retail.

## Read-through for Snap

Klarna's aggressive vertical expansion into auto dealerships deepens its embedded-finance reach and could encroach on lease-to-own and installment players serving consumers at the point of repair or purchase.
