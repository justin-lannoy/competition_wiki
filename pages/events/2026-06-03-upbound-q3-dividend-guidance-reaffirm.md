# Upbound Group Declares $0.39 Q3 2026 Quarterly Dividend, Reaffirms FY Revenue Outlook of $4.70B–$4.95B

## What happened

Upbound Group's Board approved a $0.39 quarterly cash dividend for Q3 2026, payable July 7 to stockholders of record as of June 16, 2026. The company also reaffirmed full-year 2026 consolidated revenue guidance of $4.70B–$4.95B, and guided Q2 revenue of $1.1B–$1.2B after Q1 revenue of $1.2B came in slightly below the $1.23B consensus.

## Read-through for Snap

Flat revenue guidance and a modest Q1 revenue miss suggest Acima's growth is stabilizing rather than accelerating, limiting its ability to invest aggressively in merchant acquisition vs. Snap.
