# FirstCash Files 8-K Investor Presentation Update; AFF Merchant Network Grows to ~16,600 Active Locations

## What happened

FirstCash Holdings filed an 8-K on May 12, 2026, including an updated investor presentation. Per the most recent Q1 2026 data embedded in the filing, American First Finance (AFF) now serves approximately 16,600 active retail merchant partner locations, up from ~14,500 a year earlier — a 14% YoY increase. AFF gross revenues did decline 11% in Q1 due to merchant bankruptcies in late 2024, but charge-off rates remained stable and the segment stayed profitable. FirstCash overall posted record Q1 2026 revenue of $1.05B and EPS of $2.43.

## Read-through for Snap

AFF's expanding merchant network despite near-term revenue headwinds demonstrates active door-count competition with Snap Finance in non-prime POS retail finance, warranting monitoring of shared merchant verticals.
