# FirstCash Holdings Shareholders Approve Reincorporation from Delaware to Texas at Annual Meeting

## What happened

FirstCash Holdings held its Annual Meeting of Stockholders on June 9, 2026, where shareholders approved reincorporation from Delaware to Texas by conversion, with 23.6 million votes for and 16.8 million against. Shareholders also elected three directors for three-year terms and ratified RSM LLP as independent auditor. American First Finance (AFF), the LTO/POS financing subsidiary with 13,500+ active retail merchant partners, is unaffected operationally.

## Read-through for Snap

The reincorporation is a governance housekeeping move for parent FCFS and has no near-term impact on American First Finance's merchant network or competitive positioning against Snap Finance.
