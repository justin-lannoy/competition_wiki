# Affirm Reports Fiscal Q3 2026 Results: Revenue $1.04B (+33% YoY), GMV $11.6B (+35% YoY); Raises Full-Year Outlook

## What happened

Affirm Holdings reported fiscal Q3 2026 results with revenue of $1.04 billion (beating consensus by ~4%) and EPS of $0.30 (vs. $0.17 consensus), a 76% beat. GMV of $11.6 billion grew 35% YoY, exceeding management's own guidance range. Affirm raised full-year FY2026 GMV guidance to $49.27B–$49.57B and revenue to $4.175B–$4.205B. Active merchants grew 44% YoY to 515,000. The Affirm Card showed 146% GMV growth YoY.

## Read-through for Snap

Affirm's accelerating GMV and merchant growth at the BNPL end of the financing spectrum indicates strong consumer demand for flexible pay-over-time options, validating the broader non-prime/near-prime market Snap Finance serves.
