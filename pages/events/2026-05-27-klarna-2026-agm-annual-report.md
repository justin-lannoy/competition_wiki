# Klarna Files First Post-IPO Annual Report and Calls June 22 AGM, Highlights 22% GMV Growth in 2025

## What happened

On May 27, 2026, Klarna released its 2025 Annual Report and convened its first Annual General Meeting since listing on the NYSE, scheduled for June 22, 2026 in London as a hybrid event. The annual report disclosed 2025 GMV of $127.9 billion (+22%), 118 million active consumers, and credit losses of just 0.63% of GMV. The board is seeking substantial share allotment authority through 2030.

## Read-through for Snap

Klarna's disclosed scale—118M active consumers and 22% GMV growth—underscores its position as the dominant BNPL force, raising the competitive bar for all LTO and installment finance players including Snap Finance.
