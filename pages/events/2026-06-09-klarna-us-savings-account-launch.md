# Klarna Launches U.S. FDIC-Insured High-Yield Savings Account at 3%+ APY

## What happened

Klarna launched FDIC-insured high-yield savings accounts in the U.S. on June 9, 2026, offering APYs starting at 3.28% with no minimum deposit and no monthly fees, held by WebBank. The product is embedded directly in the Klarna app alongside its BNPL and card offerings, positioning Klarna as a full consumer financial hub. Klarna already holds $12.3 billion in deposits globally from similar products offered in Europe since 2021.

## Read-through for Snap

Klarna's savings account deepens consumer engagement and deposit-funded liquidity, giving it a structural cost-of-capital advantage over pure-play LTO/BNPL competitors like Snap Finance and potentially allowing it to cross-sell installment financing to a stickier, deposits-anchored user base.
