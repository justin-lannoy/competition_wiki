# Klarna Partners with Minor Hotels to Offer Flexible Payments Across 13 European Markets Ahead of Summer Travel Season

## What happened

Klarna announced a partnership with Minor Hotels, a global hospitality group with 640+ properties across 63 countries, to offer Klarna payment options for hotel bookings across 13 European markets. Guests can pay immediately or split payments into three interest-free installments, expanding Klarna's footprint into travel commerce ahead of the summer season.

## Read-through for Snap

Klarna's continuous expansion into new verticals and geographies underscores its scale ambitions, though the European hospitality focus has limited near-term direct impact on Snap Finance's US retail LTO market.
