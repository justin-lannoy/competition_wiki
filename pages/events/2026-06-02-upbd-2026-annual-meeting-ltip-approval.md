# Upbound Group Stockholders Approve 2026 Long-Term Incentive Plan at Annual Meeting

## What happened

At its June 2, 2026 Annual Meeting, Upbound Group shareholders approved the 2026 Long-Term Incentive Plan authorizing up to 4,590,636 shares of common stock, replacing the 2021 LTIP. Shareholders also ratified Deloitte & Touche as auditor and approved advisory say-on-pay on an annual basis. The vote reflects continued board and investor alignment on executive compensation structure.

## Read-through for Snap

Stable Upbound/Acima governance reduces near-term leadership disruption risk, keeping Acima focused on its LTO digital expansion and competitive pressure on Snap Finance.
