# Klarna Reports Strong Q1 2026: Revenue +44% YoY to $1B, Adjusted Operating Profit $68M

## What happened

Klarna reported Q1 2026 GMV of $33.7B (+33% YoY), revenue of $1.0B (+44% YoY), and adjusted operating profit of $68M—up from just $3M a year earlier. Active consumers reached 119 million (+21% YoY) and merchant count surpassed 1 million. The company reiterated full-year 2026 guidance and issued Q2 guidance of $960M–$1B in revenue.

## Read-through for Snap

Klarna's accelerating profitability and consumer engagement at massive scale signal intensifying competition for non-prime and near-prime consumer financing at the point of sale, pressuring Snap Finance's merchant acquisition and consumer conversion rates.
