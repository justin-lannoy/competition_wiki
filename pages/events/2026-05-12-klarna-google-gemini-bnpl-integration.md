# Klarna Embeds BNPL Into Google Search, AI Mode, and Gemini App via Google Pay

## What happened

Klarna announced it will bring its flexible payment options—including pay-in-four installments and longer-term financing—directly into Google's Gemini app and Google Search (including AI Mode) via Google Pay in the U.S. The integration is powered by the Universal Commerce Protocol and places Klarna at checkout within AI-driven shopping conversations.

## Read-through for Snap

Embedding BNPL inside Google's AI commerce ecosystem dramatically expands Klarna's top-of-funnel reach at the moment of consumer intent, making it harder for Snap Finance and lease-to-own operators to capture consumers who now encounter BNPL frictionlessly in AI search.
