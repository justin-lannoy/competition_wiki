# FirstCash Holdings Files Q1 2026 10-Q; Revenue Rises to $1.05B, Net Income $107.7M

## What happened

FirstCash Holdings filed its Q1 2026 10-Q with revenue of $1.05 billion and net income of $107.7 million, reflecting growth across pawn loans, scrap gold sales, and U.K. operations following the August 2025 acquisition of H&T Group. The Retail POS Payment Solutions segment, which houses American First Finance (AFF), continued to operate as a key growth vertical alongside pawn.

## Read-through for Snap

A financially healthy FirstCash parent supports continued investment in American First Finance's LTO and retail installment platform, maintaining its competitive position against Snap Finance in the non-prime point-of-sale finance market.
