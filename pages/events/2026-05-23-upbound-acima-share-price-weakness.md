# Upbound Group (Acima Parent) Stock Slides 22% Over 3 Months as Business Model Concerns Mount

## What happened

Upbound Group (UPBD) shares closed at $17.84 on May 23, 2026, reflecting an ~11% decline over the past month and ~22% decline over the past three months. Investors cited regulatory risks around Acima and tighter consumer credit trends as key concerns, even as Acima introduces a new General-Purpose Mastercard and Private Label Credit Cards through a partnership with Concora to expand its product offerings.

## Read-through for Snap

Persistent equity pressure on Upbound/Acima—driven by regulatory and credit risk concerns—suggests the LTO sector faces broader headwinds that could slow Acima's growth and open merchant opportunities for Snap Finance.
