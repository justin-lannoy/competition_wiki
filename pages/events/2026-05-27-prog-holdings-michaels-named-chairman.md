# PROG Holdings CEO Steve Michaels Named Chairman of the Board

## What happened

PROG Holdings announced that President and CEO Steve Michaels has been named to the additional position of Chairman of the Board. Ray Robinson, the prior Chairman, has been appointed Lead Independent Director. The move consolidates executive and board leadership under Michaels as PROG continues integrating its multi-product ecosystem including Progressive Leasing, Four Technologies, MoneyApp, and Purchasing Power.

## Read-through for Snap

Michaels consolidating the CEO and Chairman roles at PROG/Progressive Leasing signals long-term strategic conviction and may accelerate decision-making on competitive initiatives that directly affect Snap Finance's LTO market share.
