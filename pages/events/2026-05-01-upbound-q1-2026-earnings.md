# Upbound Group Reports Q1 2026 Results; Reaffirms 2026 Guidance Amid Macro Headwinds

## What happened

Upbound Group reported Q1 2026 revenue of $1.2 billion (up 3.7% YoY) and reaffirmed full-year 2026 guidance of $4.7B–$4.95B revenue and $500M–$535M adjusted EBITDA. Management highlighted the launch of a nationwide Amazon partnership and an exclusive checkout agreement with a major e-commerce furniture retailer, both expected to drive Acima GMV in H2 2026. Acima's underwriting was deliberately tightened in Q1 in response to macro headwinds.

## Read-through for Snap

Acima's Amazon distribution deal and tightened underwriting signal it is trading near-term volume for credit quality — a dynamic that may pressure Snap Finance merchants to fill approval gaps Acima steps back from.
