# Klarna Partners with Lands' End, Tekion, Worldline, and Arrive in Multi-Channel Expansion Push

## What happened

Between May 19–28, 2026, Klarna announced a series of new partnerships: a framework agreement with Worldline to embed Klarna across online and in-store checkpoints; a deal with Tekion to bring BNPL into U.S. dealership service lanes; a Lands' End integration powered by Stripe for U.S. retail; and a parking payment partnership with Arrive (EasyPark) across 15 European markets. These moves extend Klarna into automotive, parking, and lifestyle retail verticals beyond its traditional e-commerce base.

## Read-through for Snap

Klarna's rapid multi-vertical merchant network expansion deepens its consumer touchpoint density and merchant stickiness, intensifying BNPL competitive pressure on Snap Finance's retail partners who may encounter Klarna at checkout.
