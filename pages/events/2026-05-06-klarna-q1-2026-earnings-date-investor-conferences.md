# Klarna Schedules Q1 2026 Earnings for May 14 and Announces May Investor Conference Appearances

## What happened

Klarna confirmed it will publish Q1 2026 earnings on May 14, 2026, before market open, with a webcast at 8:30 a.m. ET. The company also announced CEO Sebastian Siemiatkowski will present at a May 28 investor conference and CFO Niclas Neglen at a May 20 event. Analyst consensus estimates Q1 revenue of ~$945M.

## Read-through for Snap

Klarna's Q1 results next week will provide a near-real-time read on BNPL consumer demand and credit trends directly relevant to Snap Finance's non-prime customer base.
