# PROG Holdings Reports Strong Q1 2026; Progressive Leasing GMV Returns to Growth in March

## What happened

PROG Holdings reported Q1 2026 consolidated revenue of $742.7M (+11.1% YoY) and non-GAAP EPS of $1.24, beating the Street estimate of $0.81. Progressive Leasing GMV declined 2.2% YoY to $393M overall but returned to low single-digit YoY growth in March. The company raised its full-year 2026 revenue guidance to $3.0B–$3.1B and highlighted improved portfolio yield and macro resilience.
