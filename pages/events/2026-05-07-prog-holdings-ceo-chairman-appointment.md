# PROG Holdings CEO Steve Michaels Elevated to Board Chairman; Ray Robinson Named Lead Independent Director

## What happened

PROG Holdings announced that its Board of Directors appointed President and CEO Steve Michaels to the additional role of Chairman of the Board, effective immediately. Outgoing Chairman Ray Robinson was named Lead Independent Director. The move consolidates strategic and governance leadership under Michaels as PROG executes its 2026 roadmap following a strong Q1 earnings beat.

## Read-through for Snap

Concentrated leadership at PROG Holdings may accelerate decision-making on Progressive Leasing's technology and merchant expansion initiatives, increasing competitive pressure on Snap Finance.
