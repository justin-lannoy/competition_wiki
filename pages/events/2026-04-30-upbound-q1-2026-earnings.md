# Upbound Group Reports Q1 2026 Earnings: EPS Beats, Revenue Misses; Acima Tightens Underwriting

## What happened

Upbound Group (UPBD) reported Q1 2026 EPS of $1.08, beating estimates of $1.07, while revenue of $1.2B missed the $1.23B forecast. At the Acima segment, management revised its outlook to account for Q1 results, deliberate underwriting tightening, and continued macro headwinds. The company reaffirmed full-year 2026 guidance of $4.7B–$4.95B revenue and non-GAAP EPS of $4.00–$4.35.

## Read-through for Snap

Acima's tightened underwriting and macro-driven caution may push non-prime applicants toward Snap Finance, creating a near-term volume opportunity.
