# FirstCash Holdings Posts Strong Q1 2026 Beat; AFF Segment Continues to Support Retail POS Payment Solutions

## What happened

FirstCash Holdings (FCFS) reported Q1 2026 EPS of $2.69, beating consensus of $2.30 by $0.39, on revenue of $1.05B (+25.7% YoY), driven by pawn operations including the H&T Group UK acquisition completed in August 2025. The Retail POS Payment Solutions segment (which includes American First Finance) continued to operate within the consolidated platform. Zacks upgraded FCFS to 'Strong Buy' on May 2, 2026 following the strong print.

## Read-through for Snap

FirstCash's robust parent-company financial health provides AFF with a strong balance sheet to invest in lease-to-own merchant expansion, intensifying competitive pressure on Snap Finance.
