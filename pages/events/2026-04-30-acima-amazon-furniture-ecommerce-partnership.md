# Acima Launches Nationwide Amazon Partnership and Exclusive E-Commerce Furniture Checkout Deal

## What happened

During Upbound's Q1 2026 earnings call, management highlighted the launch of a nationwide Amazon partnership and an exclusive checkout agreement with the largest e-commerce furniture retailer as substantial strategic steps for Acima. These deals are expected to enhance customer traffic, brand reach, and Acima's GMV in the second half of 2026.

## Read-through for Snap

Acima's expanded digital retail footprint via Amazon and a leading furniture e-tailer intensifies competition with Snap Finance for non-prime consumers shopping online.
