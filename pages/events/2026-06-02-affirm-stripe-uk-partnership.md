# Affirm Expands Stripe Partnership to UK Market, Bringing Pay-Over-Time to British Merchants

## What happened

Affirm and Stripe announced an expanded partnership on June 2, 2026, that will bring Affirm's pay-over-time solutions to Stripe merchants in the UK for the first time, with British businesses able to add Affirm to their online checkout starting in July 2026. The deal extends an already-established U.S. and Canada Stripe integration into a major new international market. This follows Affirm's strong FQ3 2026 results (GMV +35% to $11.6B, revenue +33% to $1.04B) and its full-year GMV guidance of ~$49.3–$49.6B.

## Read-through for Snap

Affirm's accelerating international distribution through Stripe's massive merchant base deepens its BNPL moat and signals growing appetite from prime/near-prime consumers for installment options, which could pressure Snap Finance's addressable market as merchants standardize on Affirm-powered checkout flows.
