# Michigan AG Secures $3.25M Settlement With Koalafi Over Lease-to-Own Violations

## What happened

Michigan Attorney General Dana Nessel secured an Assurance of Voluntary Compliance and Discontinuance with West Creek Financial d/b/a Koalafi, resolving allegations that the company violated the Michigan Consumer Protection Act and Rental-Purchase Agreement Act. Koalafi agreed to pay $3.25M—primarily for restitution to Michigan consumers who entered auto-repair lease-to-own agreements. The investigation began in 2024 after complaints that LTO agreements were being used for auto-repair transactions including labor and fluids.
