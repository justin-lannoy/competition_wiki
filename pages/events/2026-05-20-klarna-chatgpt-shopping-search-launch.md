# Klarna Launches AI-Powered Shopping Search App Inside ChatGPT

## What happened

On May 20, 2026, Klarna launched a Shopping Search app directly inside ChatGPT, enabling real-time product discovery within conversational AI. The launch targets the rapid growth of AI-driven retail traffic, which grew nearly 700% during the 2025 holiday season with 31% higher conversion rates from AI-referred shoppers. This embeds Klarna's payment options at the pre-purchase discovery stage.

## Read-through for Snap

Klarna's AI-embedded commerce push positions it earlier in the consumer purchase funnel, potentially reducing the pool of non-prime shoppers who reach checkout stages where Snap Finance and LTO products compete.
