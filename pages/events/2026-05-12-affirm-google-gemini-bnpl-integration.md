# Affirm Embeds BNPL Into Google Pay, Gemini App, and Google Search AI Mode

## What happened

Affirm announced that its pay-over-time options will be integrated into Google Pay across Google Search (including AI Mode) and the Gemini app, placing Affirm alongside Klarna within Google's AI-powered shopping ecosystem in the U.S. The move drove Affirm's stock up ~6% on May 26–27 as the integration's consumer impact became clearer.

## Read-through for Snap

Affirm securing a default position inside Google's AI commerce layer materially strengthens its prime and near-prime consumer reach, intensifying competition for the financing decision moment that Snap Finance relies on at the merchant level.
